Find ICD-10 codes mapped to a CPT/HCPCS code, or vice versa, across Medicare coverage articles

How this works

Code mappings are sourced from Medicare "Billing & Coding" articles that accompany Local Coverage Determinations (LCDs). Each article defines which CPT/HCPCS procedure codes are covered for specific ICD-10 diagnosis codes. Results show the coverage context from the originating article and LCD.
